flowchart TD
    A[Start with a Core Idea] --> B[Define Genre & Tone]
    B --> C[Create Main Characters]
    C --> D[Build the World]
    D --> E[Establish Conflict]

    E --> F[Outline Plot Structure]

    F --> G1[Beginning]
    F --> G2[Middle]
    F --> G3[Ending]

    G1 --> H1[Introduce Characters & Stakes]
    G2 --> H2[Escalate Conflict]
    G3 --> H3[Resolve Transformation]

    H1 --> I[Write First Draft]
    H2 --> I
    H3 --> I

    I --> J[Add Dialogue & Sensory Detail]
    J --> K[Refine Character Motivations]
    K --> L[Edit for Pacing & Clarity]

    L --> M{Does the Story Work?}

    M -- No --> N[Revise Plot, Characters, or Setting]
    N --> I

    M -- Yes --> O[Polish Language & Style]
    O --> P[Final Manuscript]
    P --> Q[Publish or Share Story]
